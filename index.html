Gia Marie (YT)

Gia Marie (23) is a self taught Video Editor and Creator.
When Gia was 17, she discovered she had a natural talent
for video editing and began doing it as a hobby.
Shortly after, within the next year, she knew she wanted
to pursue video editing as a career.

In her senior year of highschool, she attended a
part time arts school in Trumbull, Connecticut, Regional
Center for the Arts. (RCA) Gia has been video editing ever
since and has gained even more creative interests since attending
Housatonic Community College in Bridgeport, Connecticut.

What is GiaMarieYT?

"giamarieyt" is simply a username. In highschool, "giamarieyt"
was and still is the username of my youtube channel and then instagram.
The name stuck with my friends and peers and even became a nickname I get
called by many of them.
